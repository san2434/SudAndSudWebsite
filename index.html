Our Team

K.K. Sud - Founder

K.K. Sud, a revered Senior Advocate of the Supreme Court of India, was celebrated as one of the leading legal minds of his generation. His career was defined by a deep commitment to justice and patriotism. During the Emergency of 1977, he played a crucial role in securing the release of several political leaders who were detained under spurious charges. Despite personal risks, including an arrest warrant, he continued his advocacy, demonstrating unmatched courage and resilience.

Notable achievements include:

  • Representation in the Babri Masjid case, securing bail for leaders such as L.K. Advani and Murali Manohar Joshi.
  • Defense in the Jain Hawala Diaries case, leading to acquittals and quashing allegations against senior BJP figures.
  • Appointed as Additional Solicitor General of India until 2003.

His tenure revitalized the Adhivakta Parishad, cementing his legacy as a leader and mentor within the legal community.

Mr. Jayant K. Sud - Senior Advocate

Mr. Jayant K. Sud, a Senior Advocate, has significant experience in the Supreme Court of India and the Delhi High Court. Known for his expertise in criminal law and handling high-profile cases, he served as Additional Solicitor General of India from 2020 to 2023 and as Additional Advocate General for Punjab from 2012 to 2017.

Notable achievements include:

  • Representation of the Piramal Group in major cases before the National Company Law Appellate Tribunal (NCLAT).
  • Oversaw national-level elections as the Returning Officer for Special Olympics Bharat.

His career is marked by strategic insight, dedication to justice, and commitment to civic responsibilities.

Sai Manik Sud - The New Generation

Sai Manik Sud brings a modern and progressive approach to Sud and Sud, Advocates & Solicitors. As a dual-qualified lawyer with an LLB and a Master's degree from London and a bridge course at NLU Delhi, he offers a unique perspective that spans both international and domestic legal frameworks.

His presence in the firm ensures that the values and high standards set by his predecessors are maintained while integrating forward-thinking strategies tailored for the evolving legal landscape.
